Recommended CJA KPIs:
- Delivery Rate
- Open Rate
- Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- Unsubscribe Rate (by channel)
- Consent Propagation Latency (time from opt-out to AEP update)
- Segment Overlap (how many users in multiple segments)
- Profile Stitching Coverage (% of events mapped to a profileId)
- Revenue per Send (if transaction data allowed)
